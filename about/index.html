About me

A web developer turned project manager for growing startups with a passion for building great software development and customer support teams. Lucky to be able to do so as Project & Product Manager at Briefd and Platform Delivery Lead at Hiveterminal, and occasionally contribute to open-source software at night.

My journey into tech has largely been a happy accident. I had learned to tweak WordPress websites around 2006, gone on to develop websites for friends and clients and went into tech full-time in 2016, first as a developer and later as a project manager. This is when I first encountered and fell in love with Laravel. I have since contributed minor fixes and changes to many open source projects in my spare time.

I consider myself no expert, but rather believe that hard work and discipline trump talent. I am willing to offer a hand to the new developers and project managers coming into the tech industry.

I also have immense luck in working in an environment, where how we get there is equally as important as the goal. This allows me to spend days thinking about the business processes and conversations and turning them into semi-automated systems of management. I have turned many of those lessons into meetup talks, conference talks and motivational speeches. I plan to expand on this soon on the website you are currently reading.

When I'm not working (which is rare, but it happens), I take pleasure in the simple things. I enjoy reading to my son Leo and spending time with my family. I enjoy a good football game, cheering for Philadelphia Eagles and Texas Longhorns football teams. I also play the ukulele and am in the (endless) process of learning to play the guitar.

I hope to one day become better at writing code and half-decent at flying airplanes.
